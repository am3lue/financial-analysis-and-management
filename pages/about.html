What is FinTrack?

FinTrack is a powerful yet simple financial management application designed to help individuals track their income and expenses, analyze spending patterns, and make better financial decisions.

With an intuitive interface and powerful reporting features, FinTrack makes it easy to stay on top of your finances without the complexity of traditional accounting software.

How to Use

Step-by-step guide to get started

1

Navigate to Operating Page

Click on the "Operating" tab in the navigation menu. This is where you'll input all your financial data.

2

Enter Daily Income

For each day of the week, enter your income amounts in the "Income" field and click "Add" to record them. Your data is automatically saved.

3

Record Your Expenses

Similarly, enter your daily expenses in the "Expense" fields. You can add multiple entries per day as needed.

4

Add Extra Expenses

Use the "Extra Expenses" section to record any additional expenses like groceries, entertainment, or unexpected purchases. Enter a description and amount, then click "Add".

5

Monitor Your Progress

Watch the live summary cards at the top of the page to see your total income, expenses, and net profit in real-time.

6

Generate Reports

Click "Generate Weekly Report" to see a detailed analysis of your finances, including best and worst performing days.

7

End the Week

When ready to start a new week, click "End Week & Reset". This saves your data to history and clears the current week's entries.

8

View Detailed Reports

Visit the "Reports" page for comprehensive charts and visualizations of your financial data.

Tips for Success

  • Be consistent: Enter your data daily for the most accurate picture of your finances.
  • Categorize extras: Use clear descriptions for extra expenses to identify spending patterns.
  • Review weekly: Check your reports regularly to spot trends and adjust your budget.
  • Export data: Use the export feature to back up your financial records.
  • Use on any device: Your data syncs automatically across all your devices through browser storage.

‚ùì FAQ

Is my data secure?

Yes! Your data is stored locally in your browser. We never access or store your financial information on our servers.

What if I close the browser?

No problem! All data is automatically saved to localStorage and will be restored when you return.

Can I export my data?

Yes! Go to the Reports page and click "Export Data" to download your financial records as a JSON file.
